Last updated: 19th April 2026

Welcome to Chanakya Niti. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Your Rights

Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or mobile identifiers for analytics. You can manage these via your device's privacy settings, though this may affect some app features.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. No system is completely secure; in the event of a breach we will take prompt steps to mitigate impact.

Children's Privacy

Chanakya Niti is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware of any such collection, we will delete it immediately.
