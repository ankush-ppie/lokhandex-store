Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19th April 2026

Welcome to Birthday & Event Reminder. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We do not require login and do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or contact details. We may collect limited data to operate and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Device type, OS version, screens viewed, and time spent in the app.
  • Analytics: Anonymised data via Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is linked to this data.
  • Approximate Location: Used only for content relevance (e.g., regional holidays). Precise location is never collected or stored.
  • Local App Data: All birthdays, events, and reminders you create are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or access this data remotely.

App Permissions

To deliver its core features, the app requires the following device permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you timely birthday and event reminder alerts. This is the core function of the app and is required for reminders to work.
  • Storage: To save your birthdays, events, and preferences locally on your device. All data remains on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time via your device Settings → Apps → Birthday & Event Reminder → Permissions. Revoking a permission may affect the related app feature.

How We Use Your Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app's features and performance.
  • Send you scheduled reminder notifications for birthdays and events.
  • Display advertisements to support free access to the app.
  • Analyse anonymised usage data via Firebase Analytics to fix issues and improve functionality.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

Data Storage & Retention

All birthday, event, and reminder data you enter is stored locally on your device using the device's internal storage. We do not maintain any server-side database of your personal data.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device. You can also clear all app data at any time via Settings → Apps → Birthday & Event Reminder → Clear Data.

Anonymised analytics data collected by Firebase is retained per Google's standard data retention policy and is not linked to any individual user.

Advertisements

Birthday & Event Reminder displays ads via Google AdMob to support free access to the app. Third-party advertisers may use tracking technologies (such as your device's Advertising ID) to show relevant ads based on your interests.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → turn off Personalised Ads.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Obligation: When required by law, court order, or a government authority.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties such as Google (Firebase Analytics, AdMob) who are bound by confidentiality agreements and may only use data for the purposes we specify.
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, data may transfer to the new entity under the same privacy protections.

Your Rights

  • Access & Correction: Request access to or correction of any data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data. Since all reminder data is stored locally, uninstalling the app or using "Clear Data" removes it entirely.
  • Opt-out of Tracking: Manage ad tracking preferences through your device's privacy or ad settings.
  • DPDP Act (India): Users in India have rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Contact us to exercise any applicable rights.
  • GDPR (EU/EEA): EU/EEA users may exercise rights including data access, portability, and objection. Contact us to submit a request.

Cookies & Tracking

As a mobile app, we do not use browser cookies. However, we and our advertising partners (Google AdMob) may use your device's Advertising ID for analytics and ad personalisation purposes.

You can reset or opt out of your device Advertising ID at any time via your device privacy settings. Doing so may result in less relevant ads but will not affect core app functionality.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect any data we handle. Since your event and reminder data is stored locally on your device, its security also depends on your own device security (lock screen, encryption, etc.).

No system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach affecting our services, we will take prompt steps to mitigate impact and notify affected users as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Birthday & Event Reminder is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, please contact us at hello@baalshakha.com.

Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites or integrate third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob, Firebase). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable legal requirements. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may also notify you within the app.

Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page from time to time.
